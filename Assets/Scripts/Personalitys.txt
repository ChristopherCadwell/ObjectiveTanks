Personality List.

Full:
Patrols around wps looking for player (Assignable patrol method via dropdown)
when player is spotted, chases and fires
if damaged enough, attempts to run away and heal
when health is restored, goes back to patrolling and looking for player

Faker:
Alternates between chase and flee based on time

Kamikaze:
Chase down player and shoot till one of you is dead

Ambusher:
Patrols around wps, when it sees you, tries to get in front of you, and fires.
